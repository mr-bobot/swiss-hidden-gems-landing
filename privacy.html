The site loads tracking and chat tools from the following providers. Each sets cookies or similar identifiers when you interact with the page.

ManyChat pixel

We use the ManyChat pixel to connect this website with our Instagram DM funnel. When you arrive from one of our DMs, ManyChat can recognise you and attribute which conversation brought you here. The pixel sets cookies from the manychat.com and mccdn.me domains.

Privacy details: manychat.com/privacy.html

Facebook / Meta pixel

We use the Meta Pixel (operated by Meta Platforms Ireland Ltd.) to measure how visitors interact with the site, retarget people who have shown interest, and improve ads on Facebook and Instagram. The pixel sets cookies from facebook.com.

Privacy details: facebook.com/privacy/policy

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal data we hold about you. Send an email to office@gorped.com and we'll handle it within a reasonable time.

Cookies

You can block cookies in your browser settings. Blocking the cookies above may prevent some functionality (for example, email delivery of the free sample) but will not affect the rest of the site.
